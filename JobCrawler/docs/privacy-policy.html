Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

JobCrawler - Smart Job Finder ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Email address — used to create your account and send job match alerts.
  • Resume (PDF) — parsed into plain text on our server to score job relevance. The original PDF is not stored.
  • Job preferences — target roles, preferred locations, and selected companies you provide.

2. Data We Store Locally

The Extension uses chrome.storage.local to store only your account ID and email on your device, so you stay logged in between sessions. No other personal data is stored locally.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Match job listings against your resume and preferences using AI-based relevance scoring.
  • Send email alerts when high-relevance jobs are found.
  • Display personalized job recommendations in the Extension popup.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your data is only used to provide the job matching service. We use the following service providers to operate:

  • MongoDB Atlas — database hosting
  • Azure OpenAI — resume-job relevance scoring
  • SendGrid — email delivery

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as you use the service. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

All communication between the Extension and our servers uses HTTPS encryption. API access is protected by authentication keys.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us at the address below.
